Why freelancers look past Billdu

Billdu is a capable invoicing app and platform, but for a solo freelancer it can be more than the job needs: an account, a web app, and a recurring plan to do what is, at its core, sending invoices to clients.

InvoicePro takes the opposite bet. It is a single file that runs offline, keeps your client list and invoice history on your own device, and never asks you to log in or subscribe. You trade cloud features you may not use for privacy, simplicity, and ownership.

Frequently asked questions

Is InvoicePro really free?
The core tool is free to use with no account. Pro adds extra features as a one-time purchase, not a subscription.
Does it work offline like Billdu doesn't?
Yes. InvoicePro runs entirely on your device with no internet required, whereas Billdu is a cloud service.
Can it replace Billdu for a team?
Not really. InvoicePro is built for solo freelancers. If you need team collaboration and a full platform, Billdu is the better fit.
Where is my data stored?
On your own machine, in a file you own. It never goes to a vendor's servers.
Do I lose my data if I stop paying?
No. Because the file is local and yours, there is no subscription that can lock you out of your own records.
