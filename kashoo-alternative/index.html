Why people look for a Kashoo alternative

Kashoo is accounting software, which makes sense if you need a cloud ledger and accounting features. For many freelancers, the immediate need is smaller: see income, expenses, profit, and a tax set-aside without turning it into a platform.

CashFlow is a local, single-purpose tracker. It helps you record money in and out, keep a clearer monthly picture, and avoid sending your financial notes into another cloud account. Pro is $12 one time.

No. It can show a tax set-aside estimate for planning, but it is not tax advice or filing software.

On your own device. It is built for freelancers who do not want financial notes stored in another cloud account.
