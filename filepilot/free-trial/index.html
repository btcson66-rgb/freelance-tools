Good trial folders

The most useful test is a folder with mixed, imperfect files:

  • A few normal PDF invoices with selectable text.
  • A few scanned receipts or phone photos.
  • One or two bank documents, contracts or quotes.
  • At least one duplicate file and one unclear scan, so you can see Duplicates and Needs Review behavior.
