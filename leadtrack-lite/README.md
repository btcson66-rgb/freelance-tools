# LeadTrack Lite — Free Offline Freelance CRM (no signup, no subscription)

A tiny **sales-pipeline CRM for freelancers** that runs in your browser. One HTML file.
No account, no install, no subscription, and **100% offline** — your client list never
leaves your computer.

👉 **[Download `LeadTrack-Lite.html`](LeadTrack-Lite.html)** — double-click it, add a lead, done.

![LeadTrack pipeline example](screenshot.png)

## Why this exists

Every CRM wants a monthly fee and your client list in their cloud. For a freelancer
juggling a handful of deals, that's overkill — and a privacy trade you don't need to make.
This is the opposite: a single file you own forever, that works on a plane, and uploads
**nothing** anywhere.

## Features (free version)

- ✅ Visual pipeline: **Lead → Contacted → Proposal → Won / Lost**
- ✅ Live dashboard: open pipeline, **weighted forecast**, won, win rate
- ✅ **Follow-up reminders** — overdue deals flagged in red, soonest first
- ✅ Pipeline-by-stage breakdown
- ✅ Deal value, win %, source & notes per contact
- ✅ Filter by stage + instant search
- ✅ 12 currencies · English + 繁體中文
- ✅ Auto-saves in your browser — zero network requests
- ✅ Free for up to **15 deals**

## How to use (30 seconds)

1. Download `LeadTrack-Lite.html`.
2. Double-click to open it in any browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari).
3. Click **Sample data** to see it filled in, or **+ Add lead** to start.
4. Set a **Next follow-up** date on each deal so you never forget to chase it.

## Privacy

This app makes **no network requests**. Everything runs locally in your browser and is
stored only in your browser's local storage. You can verify this in your browser's
Network tab. Back up anytime via CSV export (a Pro feature) or by copying the file.
