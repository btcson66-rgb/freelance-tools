The best free freelance tools in 2026

Organised by the job it does — invoicing, time, clients, contracts, money and rate-setting. The picks below all run in your browser with no account and nothing uploaded, and we name the popular cloud alternatives honestly so you can choose what fits.

"Free" can mean two very different things. Most free freelance software is free cloud software: genuinely useful, but it still wants an account, keeps your client data on its servers, and is usually funded by upsells. The other kind of free is offline and account-free — a tool that lives on your computer, makes zero network requests, and never sees your data. This guide leans toward the second kind, because for a freelancer that means privacy and no lock-in. Where a popular cloud option is the better fit, we say so.

Best free invoicing tool

Offline / no-account pick

InvoicePro — free invoice generator

A single web page that makes professional invoices and quotes with your logo, tax, discounts and 12 currencies, then prints to PDF. No sign-up, nothing uploaded. See how it stacks up against FreshBooks, Wave and Zoho Invoice.

Popular free cloud alternatives: Wave (free invoicing + accounting with payments), Zoho Invoice (free, inside the Zoho ecosystem). Choose these if you want online payment collection and access from any device with a login.

Best free freelance CRM

Best free contract & proposal maker

Best free expense & tax tracker

How we picked

Every offline pick here meets the same bar: it works in any modern browser, needs no account, makes zero network requests, keeps your data on your computer, and is either free or a one-time purchase rather than a subscription. The popular cloud alternatives we name are legitimately good — they simply make a different trade, keeping your data in their cloud in exchange for syncing, payments and team features. Pick based on what matters more to you: hands-off cloud convenience, or owning private tools outright.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best free invoicing tool for freelancers?
For free cloud invoicing with payments, Wave and Zoho Invoice are strong. For a no-account tool that keeps data on your computer, InvoicePro makes invoices and quotes offline.
Are there free freelance tools that do not require an account?
Yes — the offline, single-file tools here all run in your browser with no sign-up and nothing uploaded. Most cloud tools, even free ones, require an account.
Is free freelance software safe for client data?
Cloud tools store your data on their servers, so safety depends on their security. Offline tools that make zero network requests keep everything local, so there is no server to breach.
