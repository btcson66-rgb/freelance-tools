Why freelancers look past Wave

Wave offers genuinely free accounting, so this isn't a cheaper-than pitch — it's an ownership one. Wave is a cloud service: your income, expenses, and customer data sit on their servers behind an account, and access depends on that account and the company's business model continuing.

CashFlow keeps your finances local. Income and expense tracking, running profit, and tax set-aside all live in a single offline file on your own device. No account, no cloud, nothing that can change terms or disappear. For freelancers who'd rather their financial records simply be theirs, that's the point.
