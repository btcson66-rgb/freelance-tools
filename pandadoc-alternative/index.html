Why freelancers look past PandaDoc

PandaDoc automates documents at scale — templates, e-signatures, CRM integrations, approval workflows — sold by subscription with your documents in its cloud. It's built for sales orgs processing lots of paperwork. A solo freelancer writing the occasional proposal and contract rarely needs that, and may not want client documents hosted externally.

ProposalPro is the lightweight, owned counterpart: a single offline file for building proposals and service contracts, no account, one-time price. Everything stays on your device, and there's no platform to learn or rent.
