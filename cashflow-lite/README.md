# CashFlow Lite — Free Offline Income & Expense Tracker (no signup, no subscription)

A tiny **income/expense & tax tracker for freelancers** that runs in your browser. One HTML file.
No account, no install, no subscription, **100% offline** — your data never leaves your computer.

👉 **[Download `CashFlow-Lite.html`](CashFlow-Lite.html)** — double-click, add a transaction, done.

## Features (free version)
- ✅ Income + expense transactions
- ✅ Live dashboard: income / expenses / net profit / tax to set aside
- ✅ Monthly income-vs-expense chart + category breakdown
- ✅ Paid / Pending status, year/month/type filters + search
- ✅ 12 currencies · English + 繁體中文
- ✅ Auto-saves locally — zero network requests
- ✅ Free for up to **25 transactions**
