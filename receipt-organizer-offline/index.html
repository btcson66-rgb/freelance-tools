Why offline matters for receipts

Receipt folders can contain vendor names, payment amounts, bank details, tax IDs and client context. If you do not want those files uploaded to a cloud receipt app or cloud AI API, the safer pattern is local extraction and local filing.

FilePilot Local's privacy model

PDF text extraction and OCR run on your Windows computer. Scanned PDFs, receipt photos and business documents are not uploaded. The app does not use a cloud AI API.

What gets organized

Receipts

Scanned receipts, phone photos and PDF receipts can be OCRed in Traditional Chinese and English.

Business files

Receipt-specific details FilePilot can read

  • Date, company, amount and document type.
  • NT$ amounts and Chinese-numeral amounts.
  • ROC dates used in Taiwan documents.
  • 8-digit Taiwan tax IDs with checksum.
  • Taiwan invoice numbers plus regular English receipt formats.
