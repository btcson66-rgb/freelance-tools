Why freelancers look past Zoho Books

Zoho Books is genuine accounting software — ledgers, reconciliation, reports, the whole apparatus — delivered as a cloud service with an account and plans (including a free tier in some regions). For a freelancer whose real need is sending clean invoices and quotes, that's a lot of system, with your financial data hosted on Zoho's servers.

InvoicePro is built for the invoicing job alone. It's a single offline file that produces professional invoices and quotes you print straight to PDF, with no account and no subscription. Your client and billing data stays entirely on your device.
