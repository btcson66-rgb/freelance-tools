Powerful accounting vs. the three numbers that matter

Xero earns its reputation as a full-featured cloud accounting platform. For a solo freelancer billing a handful of clients, it can feel like industrial machinery for a small workshop: a monthly subscription, bank-feed syncing, a chart of accounts, and an interface optimized for bookkeepers.
