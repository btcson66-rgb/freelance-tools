Why freelancers look past 17hats

17hats is an all-in-one business platform — CRM, invoicing, contracts, scheduling, bookkeeping — sold as a subscription. If you use every module it's convenient, but many freelancers pay for the whole suite to use a fraction of it, with their client data living in 17hats' cloud.

LeadTrack does one job: it's a freelance CRM with a weighted pipeline so you can see which deals are likely to close. It runs offline, keeps your client data on your device, and costs a one-time fee. You pair it with separate, focused free tools for invoices and contracts rather than renting a megasuite.
