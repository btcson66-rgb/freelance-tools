The CRM that doesn't need an onboarding call

HubSpot, Pipedrive and Streak are built for sales teams — seats, integrations, automation rules and a monthly bill. As a freelancer you mostly need to answer one question every morning: who do I chase today? This tool answers exactly that, and nothing more. Open it and you have a working pipeline with no setup.

Add a deal with a contact, company, value and where it came from. It moves through five stages — Lead → Contacted → Proposal → Won/Lost — and each stage auto-sets a sensible win probability you can override. The dashboard shows open pipeline value, a weighted forecast (so you plan against realistic revenue, not best case), won totals and your win rate. A follow-ups panel keeps the next action in front of you so warm leads don't go cold.

  1. Add your open deals with a contact, value and source.
  2. Set a follow-up date and move each deal along as it progresses.

Frequently asked questions

Is this CRM free?
Yes. The free version manages your full pipeline with no account. An optional one-time Pro version lifts the deal cap and enables CSV export.
Is it actually simple, or another bloated CRM?
Deliberately minimal: five stages, each deal with a contact, value and follow-up date. No integrations, no team seats, no setup wizard.
How does the weighted forecast work?
Each open deal has a win probability (auto-filled by stage or set by hand). The forecast sums value × win% for a realistic expected-revenue number.
Will it remind me to follow up?
Yes. Open deals are listed soonest-first by follow-up date, with overdue flagged red and today's in amber.
Where is my client data stored?
Only on your computer, in local storage. Zero network requests — your leads are never uploaded.
