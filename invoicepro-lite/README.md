## How to use (30 seconds)

1. Download `InvoiceGenerator-Lite.html`.
2. Double-click to open it in any browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari).
3. Fill in your details and line items.
4. Click **Save PDF** → choose *Save as PDF* in the print dialog.
