Why freelancers look past Agiled

Agiled is a broad business-management platform — CRM, projects, finance, HR, contracts — on a subscription. That breadth suits growing agencies, but for a solo freelancer it can mean paying for and navigating modules you'll never touch, with everything stored in Agiled's cloud.

LeadTrack is the narrow, owned alternative: a weighted-pipeline CRM that lives as a single offline file on your device. It tracks leads and clients, shows you likely revenue, and never asks for an account or a recurring payment.
