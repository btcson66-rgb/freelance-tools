Why freelancers look past Hubstaff

Hubstaff is designed for employers who want oversight — screenshots, activity levels, GPS, and dashboards to watch a workforce. For a freelancer tracking their own time, that surveillance machinery is irrelevant at best and uncomfortable at worst, and it all runs through an account and a subscription.

TimeTrack carries none of it. It's a single offline file with a one-click timer, no screenshots, no activity scoring, no cloud. You track your billable hours for your own invoicing, and the record never leaves your device.
