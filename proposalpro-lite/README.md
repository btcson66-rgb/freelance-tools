# ProposalPro Lite — Free Offline Proposal & Contract Maker (no signup, no subscription)

A tiny **proposal + service-contract maker for freelancers** that runs in your
browser. One HTML file. No account, no install, no subscription, and **100%
offline** — your client agreements never leave your computer.

👉 **[Download `ProposalPro-Lite.html`](ProposalPro-Lite.html)** — double-click it, click **Sample data**, hit **Print**.

## Why this exists

Every freelance-contract tool wants a monthly fee, your email, and your client
agreements in their cloud. For a solo freelancer who just needs to send a clean
proposal and get a contract signed, that's overkill — and a privacy trade you
don't need to make. This is the opposite: a single file you own forever, that
works on a plane, and uploads **nothing** anywhere.

## Features (free version)

- ✅ Proposal, **Service Agreement (contract)**, or both in one document
- ✅ Pre-written, plain-English freelance clauses you can toggle & edit:
  payment terms, late fees, revisions, IP / ownership, confidentiality,
  independent-contractor status, cancellation / kill fee, governing law
- ✅ Scope of work + **out-of-scope** list (stops scope creep)
- ✅ Flexible pricing: fixed fee, hourly, or milestones; deposit & balance due
- ✅ Built-in client acceptance: typed signatures & dates
- ✅ Clean **Print / Save as PDF** to send your client
- ✅ 12 currencies · English + 繁體中文
- ✅ Auto-saves in your browser — zero network requests
- ✅ Free for **1 saved document**

## How to use (60 seconds)

1. Download `ProposalPro-Lite.html`.
2. Double-click to open it in any browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari).
3. Click **Sample data** to see a finished proposal + contract, or start your own.
4. Toggle the clauses you want, set your price and deposit, then **Print / Save as PDF**.

## A quick, honest note on legal

ProposalPro gives you editable, professional templates — not legal advice. The
default clauses are sensible and freelancer-friendly, and for most everyday
projects they're exactly what you need. For high-value or high-risk work, have a
lawyer review your final contract.

## Privacy

This app makes **no network requests**. Everything runs locally in your browser
and is stored only in your browser's local storage. You can verify this in your
browser's Network tab.
