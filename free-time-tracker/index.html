Track hours without renting a SaaS

Toggl, Harvest and Clockify are great — and overkill if you're one person billing a few clients. They want a login, a team plan and your data on their servers. Frequently asked questions

Is this time tracker free?
Yes. The free version tracks time and billable hours in your browser with no account. An optional one-time Pro version lifts the entry cap and removes prompts.
Does the timer keep running if I reload or close the tab?
Yes. A running timer is saved with a start timestamp, so reloading the page restores the correct elapsed time.

Yes. Each entry has a billable flag and its own rate; the dashboard shows tracked hours, billable hours, value and utilization.
Can I export my hours for invoicing?
Yes. Export an invoice-ready CSV that opens cleanly in Excel, Google Sheets or Numbers.
Is my data private?
Completely. Everything is stored locally with zero network requests — nothing about your projects or rates is uploaded.
