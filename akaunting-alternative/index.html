Why people look for an Akaunting alternative

Akaunting is open-source accounting software, and that is a real advantage if you want a configurable accounting system. The tradeoff is that self-hosted or hosted software can still mean setup, maintenance, accounts, updates, and a bigger workflow than a freelancer needs for basic cash visibility.

CashFlow takes the small-file route. It is not trying to undercut open source on price. It is for the freelancer who wants income, expenses, net profit, and a tax set-aside view in a private local tool with no server to run.
